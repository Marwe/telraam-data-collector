# Telraam Data License

All Telraam data in this repository are provided under the Creative Commons **CC BY-NC** license.
Source: https://faq.telraam.net/article/9/telraam-data-license-what-can-i-do-with-the-telraam-data

## What you can do

- Use, adapt, and publish the data for **non-commercial** purposes.
- Share analyses, visualizations, and derivatives as long as you provide attribution to Telraam and respect the non-commercial restriction.

## Commercial use

- The project and participating citizens should be rewarded fairly for commercial success.
- If you want to use Telraam data commercially, or are unsure whether your use is commercial, please contact **info@telraam.net**.

## Attribution

- Please credit “Telraam” when using or republishing the data.
